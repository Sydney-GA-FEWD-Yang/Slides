Tags give your content meaning

Some tags do the same thing visually but have very different meanings

For example <section>, <article> and <div> are visually equivalent

But an article tag refers to an entire article and a section refers to a part of that article where as a div refers to anything in general

These are especially important for any automated readers, for example search engines
